Track and Manage: Monitor the progress of your document and receive notifications when signatures are completed. Download and Store: Once all signatures are collected, download the signed document for your records Prepare Your Documents: Ensure that your documents are properly formatted and ready for e-signature before uploading them to ClixSign.
