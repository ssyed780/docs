---
description: "POST /https:/api.forthcrm.com/v1/documents
The Documents section allows users to upload various types of documents related to contacts, debts, syndications, orders, and loans along with customizable tags. Users can also utilize a sign service for electronic signatures and retrieve detailed information about the uploaded documents, signatories, and signatures. Additionally, this section provides error handling for status codes 400 and 403."
title: "Generate a Document"
