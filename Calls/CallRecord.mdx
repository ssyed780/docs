---
title: "Create a Call Record"
description: "POST /https:/api.forthcrm.com/v1/calls

The Calls section allows users to create new call records with detailed information such as call type, notes, and duration. Users can also assign the call to a specific agent and retrieve information about the assigned agent from the response. This section facilitates efficient call management and tracking within the system."
---
